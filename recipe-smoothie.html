🥤 Protein Smoothie

🍳 Snack Recipe  |  Healthy & Delicious

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Protein powder
  • Almond milk
  • Peanut butter
  • Ice cubes
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend on high for 30–45 seconds.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy.
